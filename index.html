A Journey of David's Coding

About the creator:
David Nguyen is a 19 year old studying a Bachelor's of Software Engineering and Computer Science. He is currently in his third year and plans to graduate by 2022 (Hopefully!). Although it is a hefty five-year long course, David has grown to love programming, and enjoys it as a hobby as much as a potential career. Be that as it may, David pours any free time he can get into curating his skills both on the computer and in the real world to become a better programmer and a better person every day.

About this website:
This website was a product of his endeavour into web development in November of 2019. This space is a dedicated portfolio of David's projects and learning, which will grow and flourish along with his web development techniques in order to create a reflective space for all to enjoy. On that note, please enjoy :)
